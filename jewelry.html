The Brief

Luxury jewellery
deserves luxury visuals.

A complete AI-generated campaign for a fine jewellery brand — covering necklaces, rings, and bracelets. Each product category was treated as its own editorial world, unified by a shared visual language: dark studio backgrounds, warm gold light, and close-up compositions that let the craftsmanship speak.

The challenge was consistency across product types — a necklace is worn very differently to a ring. We built a per-category lighting and framing system to solve this.

Direction Note

Hands as the canvas.

Ring photography requires a different approach — the hand is as important as the piece. Skin tone, nail treatment, and finger placement were all specified in the prompt system to ensure a consistent, elegant feel across every ring shot.

Visual System

Built for consistency.

Every image in this campaign follows the same rules — so the brand feels like one world, not a collection of individual shots.

Light

Dark Studio

Near-black backdrop. Single warm key light from above-left. No hard shadows on skin. Gold and silver metals catch the light differently — handled per-product.

Composition

Tight & Intentional

Close-up framing to show craftsmanship. Minimal negative space. Every frame is cropped to draw the eye directly to the product without distraction.

Model Direction

Stillness

No movement or dynamic poses. The jewellery should feel settled, worn, owned. The model's role is to give context — not compete with the piece.
