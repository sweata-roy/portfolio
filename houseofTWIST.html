The Brief

Scent you can see.

Fragrance campaigns live or die by their ability to make you feel something before you smell anything. House of TWIST is a luxury fragrance house — their visual identity needed to feel like the perfume itself: rich, intimate, and layered.

We built a campaign system around three pillars: product photography that highlights the bottle as an object of desire, mood imagery that evokes the olfactory experience, and lifestyle visuals that place the brand in the wearer's world.

Visual Direction

Making the invisible, visible.

01

Scent Mapping

Each fragrance was translated into a visual vocabulary — notes like oud, rose, and amber were mapped to specific colours, textures, and lighting moods before prompting began.

02

Three-Tier System

Product, mood, and lifestyle each follow distinct rules but share the same warm-dark palette, ensuring every image belongs to the same campaign world.

03

Bottle as Protagonist

The bottle was treated as a luxury object in its own right — lit and composed with the same care given to fine jewellery or fashion accessories.
