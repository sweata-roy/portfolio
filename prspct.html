The Brief

Frames that define the face.

PRSPCT came with a clear identity: contemporary eyewear designed for people who see things differently. The campaign needed to feel minimal and editorial — clean product shots to anchor the line, lifestyle imagery to sell the feeling.

We structured the shoot around three pillars: product-only flat lays, model close-ups, and full lifestyle scenarios — each with its own framing logic but shared visual DNA.

How It Was Built

Process & Direction

01

Brand Positioning

PRSPCT targets the design-aware consumer. Clean lines, neutral environments, and understated styling were established before any image was generated.

02

Per-Section Framing

Product, model, and lifestyle each have distinct framing rules — but share the same colour temperature, backdrop tone, and shadow treatment for cohesion.

03

Prompt System

A structured prompt system ensured model consistency across all shots — same skin tone, hair, and features — so the campaign reads as one shoot, not many.
