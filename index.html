How I Work

The Process

Four Steps

01

Brand Discovery

Understanding the audience, tone, competitors, and positioning before touching a single prompt. Strategy is the foundation.

02

Moodboard & Direction

Curating visual references — lighting, color palette, model direction, texture — to define the exact feel before generating.

03

Prompt Engineering

Building structured JSON or natural-language prompts that are reusable, consistent, and precise. Not guesswork — a system.

04

Refinement & Delivery

Iterating on outputs with a designer's eye for composition, cropping, and light. Final assets delivered campaign-ready.

About Me

Designer. Strategist.
Fighter.

I'm Sweata — a 21-year-old AI visual designer, brand strategist, and founder of Sweata Roy, based in North-East India.

I don't just generate images. I approach each project with brand positioning first: who is this for, what feeling should it trigger, and why would someone stop scrolling? Then I build the visual system around that answer.

Outside of design, I train MMA and pursue a hybrid athlete lifestyle. That same discipline — preparation, iteration, precision — is what I bring to every creative project.
